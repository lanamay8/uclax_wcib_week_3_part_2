Recipe of the Week -- Chicken Tostadas

Fresh Chicken Tostadas

Healthy Chicken Tostadas with homemade tostada shells in about 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 corn tortillas or tostada shells
  • 3 medium tomatoes, diced
  • 4 medium avocados, diced
  • 3 tbsp red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 15 oz can black beans, drained & rinsed
  • 3 cups warm shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup feta or Cotija cheese
  • Cooking spray

Tostadas are a Mexican dish traditionally made with tortillas, seasoned beans, meat and vegetables. Here we have a fresh, healthy tostada made with colorful ingredients.

Directions

  1. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and arrange tortillas in a single layer. Spray with cooking spray and sprinkle both sides with salt. Bake at 400 degrees for 12 minutes, flipping halfway through.
  2. While tostadas are baking, make easy guacamole salsa by combining tomato, avocado, red onion, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, salt and lime juice. Get other ingredients ready.
  3. Assemble tostadas, topping each shell with chicken, guacamole, beans, and cheese. Feel free to be creative!
